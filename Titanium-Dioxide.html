Titanium Dioxide (TiO₂)

Overview

Titanium Dioxide (TiO₂) is the most widely used white pigment in the world, prized for its exceptional brightness, high refractive index, and opacity. It is an inert, non-toxic mineral that provides whiteness and hiding power to a vast range of products.

At SWASTIQ CORPORATION, we supply high-quality Rutile and Anatase grades of Titanium Dioxide, ensuring superior performance, color consistency, and UV resistance for our clients in the paints, plastics, paper, and cosmetics industries.
